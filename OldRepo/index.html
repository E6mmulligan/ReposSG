About Me

My work ethic stems from growing up on a small horse farm in PA, where feeding and caring for animals and the farm itself was a responsibility 24/7/365. My first career was being a Tennis Professional at some of the top-ranked clubs in the United States, using my work ethic to out-perform peers and staff in senior job titles. I thrive on creating goals that are difficult to achieve, and then achieving them. Helping other people enjoy life, building confidence in themselves, and realizing their fullest potential on- and off-court continues to be a passion of mine as I have migrated from Tennis Coach to Entrepreneur to Consultant to Mentor

My character is shaped from my faith, my family, and my passion to overcome challenges in life. My strengths include honesty, integrity, a drive to improve, creation of win-win scenarios for all parties involved, and a positive attitude, among others. Instead of helping 1-100 people simultaneously, like I can do while teaching tennis or running tennis events, I want to scale larger to help thousands or millions of people simultaneously, by creating Java programs that enrich lives of other people, while being scalable for growth beyond what I can imagine today, and while creating a profit for my organization. I believe that the ultimate commodities are time and health. I dream of creating efficiencies for people, via Java programming, so that people can spend more of their time healthly, shared with their loved ones.
