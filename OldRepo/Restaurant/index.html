Restaurant Description

Mountaintop Grill is the perfect setting to power-up your energy for the second half of your hike! Since 1984, our family business has been proud to serve and nourish over 300,000 families like yours! Whether you plan to dine-in with us and take in the breathtaking views of Rendezvous Mountain, or if you plan to pack a picnic basket, Mountaintop Grill and our entire staff hope that you share your hiking stories with us, and we'll share a few stories of our own too!
